# Logging in with ORCID

KeyBase uses **ORCID** for secure, password-less authentication.

## 1. Authorize Keybase
When signing in, you will be asked to authorize **Keybase**. This is our secure identity bridge. Click **"Authorize Access"** to link your ORCID iD to KeyBase.

## 2. Visibility Settings
**Our system requires a verified email address.** If your settings are too private, the login will fail.

* **Work Email:** Set to **"Everyone"** (green icon) for professional attribution.
* **Private Email:** Set to at least **"Trusted Parties"** (blue icon).
* **Important:** If your email is set to **"Only Me"** (red lock), the login will fail.

> Manage these in your [ORCID Personal Information](https://orcid.org/my-orcid).

## 3. Troubleshooting
If the page hangs or stays blank, your browser's "Privacy Shields" or Ad-blockers might be stopping the redirect. Try using an **Incognito/Private window**.
